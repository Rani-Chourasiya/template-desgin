About Company

We are highly experienced in the processes of Chlorination, Nitration, Sulfonation, Hydrogenation, Ethoxilation and Ammonolysis. We have more than 20 Specialty Chemicals in our Products basket.

We are exporting our products all over the world to more than 30 Countries. Our flagship companies have been conferred the status of “Star Export House” from Government of India. All our plants have the Certification of ISO 9001 : 2008, ISO 14001 : 2004 & OHSAS 18001 : 2007 for our Products.
